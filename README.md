# Vanilla Factions Expanded Classical No Auto Research

Adds a setting to disable the automatic research unlocks granted by the Eastern Republic senators' final achievement (Profectus perk).

## Why This Mod Exists

When you unite the Eastern Republic, your senators grant the Profectus perk, which automatically unlocks a random eligible research project every so often. Some players want the Eastern Republic's other benefits without the free research.

## Design Goal

Patch Profectus so its automatic research ticks can be toggled off from the mod settings, without touching any other Republic perk or unrelated VFE Classical behavior.

## Usage

"Disable auto research" is checked by default. Uncheck it in Mod Settings > Vanilla Factions Expanded - Classical - No Auto Research to restore vanilla behavior. Toggling it takes effect immediately, no restart needed.

## Installation

### Steam

Subscribe to all required mods and this patch.
